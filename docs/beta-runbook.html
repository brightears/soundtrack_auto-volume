Setup at the venue — in order

  1. Place & power the device where it hears the room (ideally near a speaker). Plug into USB power.
  2. Connect to D'ARK's WiFi (2.4 GHz): tap the screen at boot for "Change WiFi" (or it shows a AutoVolume‑XXXX network). On your phone, join AutoVolume‑XXXX → a setup page opens (or visit http://192.168.4.1) → pick D'ARK's 2.4 GHz network → enter password → Save. The device reconnects on its own after this.
  3. Confirm online: admin dashboard → Devices → "D'ARK EmQuartier" shows a green dot · Online with a live dB number moving.
  4. Check Soundtrack is playing on the D'ARK zone (a player must be online and playing).
  5. Go live: Configure → the D'ARK EmQuartier zone → flip the toggle ON (turns orange).
  6. Verify: make noise / wait for a busy moment → the volume number climbs; quiet → it eases back down.

⚠ Do NOT enable the zone before the device is physically at D'ARK — it would change their real music from wherever the device is.

Tuning cheat‑sheet (adjust by ear)

  • Sensitivity — how loud the room must get before volume rises. Start Medium. Maxes out too early in the evening? → Low. Barely reacts? → High.
  • Reactivity — how fast it changes. Use Slow (smooth) or Slowest. Avoid Fast/Fastest in a venue — twitchy.
  • Volume Range — min = sound when empty/quiet, max = sound when packed. Start 5–12. Not loud enough over a crowd? raise max. Too loud when empty? lower min.

If something looks off

  • Device "Offline" (red): check power + on D'ARK's 2.4 GHz WiFi. It auto‑reconnects after blips; if stuck, tap screen → Change WiFi.
  • ⚠ Player offline badge: D'ARK's Soundtrack player isn't playing → start playback in Soundtrack. Volume can't change while it's offline.
  • Online + dB moves but volume doesn't: make sure the zone toggle is ON and the player is online.
  • Volume jumpy: set Reactivity to Slow / Slowest.
