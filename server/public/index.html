Welcome to Auto-Volume

Automatically adjusts your Soundtrack Your Brand music volume based on ambient noise.

1. Connect

Power on your ESP32 device. It will create a WiFi network called AutoVolume-XXXX. Connect to it and enter your WiFi password.

2. Set Up

Once connected, your device will appear here. Use the Quick Setup wizard to link it to a Soundtrack zone.

3. Monitor

Go to the Monitor tab to see live noise levels and volume adjustments in real time.

How Auto-Volume works

The device's microphone measures ambient noise. When the room gets louder (more guests, conversations), the music volume goes up so it stays audible. When it gets quieter, the volume comes back down.

Sensitivity — How much noise is needed to trigger a change. Low = only reacts to big shifts in room noise. High = reacts to quiet conversations. Recommended: Medium
Reactivity — How quickly volume adjusts. Low = smooth gradual changes over 15-20 seconds (best for most venues). High = near-instant response. Recommended: Low-Medium
Min Volume — Quietest the music will go (0-16). Ensures music is always audible even in an empty room. Recommended: 4-5
Max Volume — Loudest the music will go (0-16). Prevents music from getting too loud. Recommended: 10-12

Reading the Monitor

dB Level — Real-time ambient noise from the microphone. Green = quiet room, Yellow = moderate noise, Red = loud.

Volume bars — Current Soundtrack volume (0-16). Updates automatically based on the noise level and your sensitivity/reactivity settings.

Pause — Temporarily stops auto-adjusting for a zone. Music keeps playing at its current volume level.

Sensitivity slider — Quick adjustment for how reactive the zone is to noise changes.
